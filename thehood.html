Welcome to Hicksville, NY

Long Island

Don't let the name fool you! Hicksville is amazing. It is a hamlet within the Town of Oyster Bay, situated in Nassau County, east of Queens, NY. You get the perks of beeing close to the best city in the world without all the hustle and bustle city life brings. Not a big town, but not a puny one either. With a population of a little over 41,000, it is a cool place to visit. Hicksville is home to one of the major Long Island Railroad stations on the Island, the best Indian/Pakistani food you can get east of Queens, and Brodway Mall. Many notable people have also come from Hicksville. Fans of 'The Sopranos' will be familiar with Hicksville alum, Dr. Melfi. And fans of the 'Piano Man' would be delighted to know, Billy Joel grew up just down the road on Meeting Lane. We even have our very own Broadway street! Hicksville is also situated just a few minutes drive from the beach. Come and visit!
